## Online Safety Checklist
- Do not click on suspicious or unknown links in emails or messages
- Never share passwords, OTPs, or personal information through email or forms
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts
- Do not talk to strangers or give them easy access to your online accounts
- Be aware of what you post and your digital footprint
- Think twice before uploading any private information or photos online
- Never share UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone
- Double-check payment requests before approving transactions
